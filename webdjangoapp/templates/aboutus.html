Why us?


At Swiveling Holidays, we are firm believers in the ability of hospitality to generate lifelong events. Our boutique hotel, which is nestled near Ireland, provides an interleaving of luxury, comfort, and individualized attention to make sure that every second of your stay is memorable.


Our hotels have been a cornerstone of elegance since its founding year, 2003. Our journey began with a goal to provide visitors a place that incorporates both cutting-edge conveniences and the welcoming personality of traditional hospitality. We have continuously been making the place better for both leisure and business tourists.


Flexible event spaces are available for hosting your special events, seminars, and celebrations. Your events at Swiveling Holidays are guaranteed to be successful thanks to the use of cutting-edge technology and the assistance of our dedicated team.


We are honored to accept the Hospitality quality Award for the third time this year, running in appreciation of our ongoing commitment to quality. This recognition highlights our ongoing dedication to our hospitality and making sure that your time with us is not wasted.
