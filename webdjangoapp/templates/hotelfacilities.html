Restaurant

Our menu features dishes from every region of the world, from the smoky flavors of Tangier to the warm scents of a Tuscan trattoria.

Bars




We present the Swiveling Holidays Bars, the bars of luxury and travel. Enter into a room where each beverage has a narrative to tell and each sip transports you to the heart of the destination.
