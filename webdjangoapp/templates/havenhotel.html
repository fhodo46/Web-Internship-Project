Haven Hotel, where


noiselessness dominates

Perfect for celibates



The Haven Hotel rises as a beacon of luxury, nestled on the picturesque sea waves of a remote bay. This lovely getaway offers coziness for tourists that seek for a change from the bustle of daily life as waves caress the shoreline.
Its 59 tastefully designed rooms and suites exhibit a fusion of contemporary elegance and coastal glamour. With modern furniture and accessories inspired by the ocean that show the surrounding beauty, each place invites visitors to unwind themselves.
...The commitment to producing culinary quality is the priority of this hotel. The waterfront restaurants of the hotel provide a variety of tastes with a focus on regional products that are obtained locally.
Guests can enjoy delicious fresh seafood dishes and artisan beverages while fully observing how coastal dining looks like.
Whether you're taking the vast sea views from a private balcony in or retiring to soft bedding after a day of visiting, everything is made in the favor of the customers.
